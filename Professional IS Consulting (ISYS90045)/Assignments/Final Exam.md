# Final Exam

## Weight

40%

## Timing

Examination period from Tuesday 9 June 2026

## Format

Written examination

## Notes

- See [[Exam Prep Reminder]] before creating revision drills, milestone exams, or full practice exam answers.

- Week 11 transcript guidance:
  - The exam is scenario-based and requires application, not just listing definitions or methodology steps.
  - All lecture material can be assessed, including guest speaker material and tutorial concepts.
  - Each question should be answered with reference to the specific case facts, characters, tensions, and constraints.
  - Naming a concept is not enough. Explain why it applies and use evidence from the scenario.
  - If asked about conflict, distinguish intragroup from intergroup conflict and justify the label with case evidence.
  - Hard-copy notes are useful only if they are organised for quick retrieval. Prepare as if it were closed book, then use notes as backup.
  - Attempt every question because a blank answer gives markers nothing to assess.
  - For technical or health problems during the exam, raise your hand and follow invigilator instructions.
