# Exam Practice Plan

Course: [[Professional IS Consulting (ISYS90045)/Course Overview|Professional IS Consulting (ISYS90045)]]

## Purpose

Use weekly quizzes for immediate recall, milestone exams for delayed retrieval after major consulting topic blocks, and full-semester practice exams for written scenario-based exam readiness.

## Adjusted Weekly Quiz Format

Weekly quizzes should keep the existing 10-question note-check score, but the question mix should prepare for applied consulting exam questions:

1. Core recall or recognition.
2. Core recall or recognition.
3. Core recall or recognition.
4. Distinguish two similar consulting concepts.
5. Distinguish two similar consulting concepts.
6. Explain why a common consulting mistake is risky.
7. Apply the week's concept to a short client scenario.
8. Apply the week's concept to a short client scenario.
9. Critique or improve weak professional advice, a proposal response, meeting plan, or recommendation.
10. Write a short closed-book exam-style answer.

Extension questions should remain separate from the 80% note-check score. Add `### Delayed Review Questions` when useful to revisit earlier weeks after a few days or weeks.

## Marking Approach

- Weekly quizzes: score only the 10 note-based questions unless otherwise requested.
- Milestone exams: mark more strictly than weekly quizzes and require specific consulting language.
- Practice exams: mark against client diagnosis, framework selection, evidence, risk, recommendation, and communication quality.
- After each attempt, record weak areas and create a focused repeat quiz if the exam-core score is below 80%.
