# Professional IS Consulting (ISYS90045)

## What Matters

- Learning outcomes
- Assessments and due dates
- Weekly seminar topics

## Intended Learning Outcomes

- ILO1: Understand the importance of the consulting process and the factors which determine its success.
- ILO2: Make use of the body of research relevant to IS consulting.
- ILO3: Elicit client expectations for a consulting assignment or service.
- ILO4: Plan a substantial information systems consulting project.
- ILO5: Communicate technical concepts to a management audience, both verbally and in writing.
- ILO6: Approach consulting problems, including people issues, in a disciplined and effective manner.
- ILO7: Work effectively in a team to collectively achieve practical consulting outcomes.

## Assessments

- AT1a: Pairs presentation, 1 or 3 minutes, 5%, starts during lecture/tutorial from 1 April 2026, within Week 5.
- AT1b: Pairs report, approximately 1,000 words, 15%, due Friday 17 April 2026 at 23:59, Week 6.
- AT2a: Group presentation, 10%, starts during lecture/tutorial from 25 May 2026, within Week 12.
- AT2b: Group written report, 30%, due Sunday 31 May 2026 at 23:59, prior to SWOTVAC.
- Final exam: Written examination, 40%, held in the examination period from Tuesday 9 June 2026.
