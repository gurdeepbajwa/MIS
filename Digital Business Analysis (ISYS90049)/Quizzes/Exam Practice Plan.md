# Exam Practice Plan

Course: [[Digital Business Analysis (ISYS90049)/Course Overview|Digital Business Analysis (ISYS90049)]]

## Purpose

Use weekly quizzes for immediate retrieval practice, milestone exams for delayed topic-block revision, and full-semester practice exams for closed-book written exam readiness.

## Adjusted Weekly Quiz Format

Weekly quizzes should keep the existing 10-question note-check score, but the question mix should be more exam-oriented:

1. Core recall or recognition.
2. Core recall or recognition.
3. Core recall or recognition.
4. Distinguish two similar concepts.
5. Distinguish two similar concepts.
6. Explain why a common misconception is wrong.
7. Apply the week's concept to a short business scenario.
8. Apply the week's concept to a short business scenario.
9. Critique or improve a weak BA answer, requirement, metric, or recommendation.
10. Write a short closed-book exam-style answer.

Extension questions should remain separate from the 80% note-check score. Add `### Delayed Review Questions` when useful to revisit earlier weeks after a few days or weeks.

## Marking Approach

- Weekly quizzes: score only the 10 note-based questions unless otherwise requested.
- Milestone exams: mark more strictly than weekly quizzes because they simulate exam conditions.
- Practice exams: mark against concept accuracy, distinction between similar terms, scenario application, and clarity of written reasoning.
- After each attempt, record weak areas and create a focused repeat quiz if the note-based or exam-core score is below 80%.
