## Quiz 1

### Note-Based Questions

1. Why does business analysis examine a need within its context rather than in isolation?

2. Which context layer includes competitive forces, industry structure, suppliers, customers, substitutes, and new entrants?
   - A. Immediate context
   - B. Organisational context
   - C. Industry context
   - D. Internal policy context

3. In PEST analysis, which factor covers interest rates, inflation, market growth, exchange rates, and cost pressures?
   - A. Political
   - B. Economic
   - C. Social
   - D. Technological

4. Name the five forces in Porter's Five Forces.

5. What is the purpose of Porter's Five Forces in context analysis?

6. Which item belongs to internal business context rather than external context?
   - A. Political stability
   - B. Industry substitutes
   - C. Organisational structure and culture
   - D. Exchange rates

7. Explain why organisational strategy matters for business analysis.

8. What does a business model describe?
   - A. How the organisation creates, delivers, and captures value.
   - B. Only the organisation's software architecture.
   - C. Only the organisation's legal compliance risks.
   - D. How competitors set prices.

9. In SWOT analysis, what is the difference between strengths and opportunities?

10. According to the Week 03 note, what should analysts avoid when using PEST and SWOT?
   - A. Using any evidence or data.
   - B. Focusing only on factors relevant to the organisation or problem area.
   - C. Analysing every possible factor without regard to relevance.
   - D. Considering trends over the next few years.

### Extension Questions

11. Harvard Business School describes Five Forces as a framework for understanding competitive forces and how economic value is divided among industry actors. How could this help a BA assess whether a proposed digital service is strategically attractive?

12. Strategyzer describes the Business Model Canvas as a simplified description of how an organisation does business and makes money. How could a BA use the canvas to test whether a proposed digital solution fits the organisation's business model?

13. SWOT analysis separates internal and external considerations. Why is it a problem if a BA lists an internal technology upgrade as a SWOT opportunity?

14. A company wants to adopt AI chatbots because competitors are doing so. Which context analysis framework would you use first, and what would you investigate?

15. Which statement best extends Week 03 context analysis into professional practice?
   - A. Context analysis should collect every possible internal and external fact before any decision can be made.
   - B. Context analysis should be purpose-driven and evidence-based, focusing on factors that materially affect the problem, opportunity, or strategic decision.
   - C. Context analysis is only needed after solution implementation.
   - D. Context analysis replaces stakeholder engagement.
