#### Extra Materials

- [What is Digital Transformation? Here is everything you need to know.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FjAq1xjBv_4) - Digital Transformation with Eric Kimberling. Connects the practice-oriented lecture theme to digital transformation as business change involving people, processes, technology, and value.
- [Digital Transformation Is Not About Technology](https://hbr.org/2019/03/digital-transformation-is-not-about-technology) - Harvard Business Review. Useful for DBA practice because it reinforces the course theme that digital transformation fails when organisations treat technology as the whole answer instead of changing people, processes, capabilities, and value delivery.
- [What is digital transformation?](https://www.mckinsey.com/featured-insights/mckinsey-explainers/what-is-digital-transformation) - McKinsey. Adds a practical management view of why transformation needs clear business outcomes, operating-model change, and adoption rather than only system implementation.
