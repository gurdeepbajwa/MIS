## Key Lessons for Assignment 2

- Include all strategic drivers from the case upfront, especially high-level business goals such as IPO/public listing if mentioned.
- Keep SWOT categories clean: opportunities and threats should be external market or environmental trends, not internal solution ideas.
- Add explanatory paragraphs after important tables, including stakeholder plans, activities, scope, risks, and matrices.
- For each BA activity, state what data it gathers and how that data supports later analysis or helps address the business problem.
- Use formal risk treatment terminology: Avoid, Transfer, Mitigate, Accept, and Increase readiness.
- Briefly explain the logic behind probability and impact ratings in risk tables.
- Keep risks focused on the business analysis phase when the assignment asks for analysis-plan risks.
- In reflection appendices, point to the exact report section or page where critical evaluation and revised thinking appear.
- In AI-use appendices, map evidence screenshots, logs, or annotations directly to the tasks listed in the contribution table.
- Keep in-text citations and final reference list formatting consistent.
