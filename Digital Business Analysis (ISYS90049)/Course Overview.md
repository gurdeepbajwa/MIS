# Digital Business Analysis (ISYS90049)

## What Matters

- Subject focus and core themes
- Intended learning outcomes
- Assessments and hurdle requirements
- Weekly lecture and tutorial topics

## Subject Focus

This subject focuses on business analysis in the context of digital technologies. It introduces modern business analysis techniques, including selected approaches from the Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK), and applies them to real business problems and opportunities using digital solutions.

## Indicative Topics

- Requirements elicitation methods, techniques, and tools
- Business analysis planning and monitoring
- Stakeholder engagement planning
- Business needs identification
- Business process identification and modelling
- Change strategy definition
- Design, delivery, and evaluation of information systems solutions

## Intended Learning Outcomes

- Describe and apply requirements gathering techniques.
- Use appropriate modelling techniques to understand a business context and the need for an information system.
- Acquire conceptual, abstraction, and analytical skills to design and analyse business problems.
- Demonstrate how to effectively document information systems solutions to business problems.
